All About Food!

As mentioned previously on my home page, outside of taking classes, I really like testing out new recipes. Generally, I like to learn how to prepare quick and healthy recipes so that I can easily feed my family and watch them thrive. When I have time, though, I try to cook traditional dishes from Turkey. Unfortunately (somewhat), when I prepare something, I prefer to eat right away. I am not the type of person to take pictures of everything I make, but you can see some of the pictures that I managed to catch before my stomach got the better of me. If you try your hand at Turkish cuisine, I’ve listed links to a few of my favorite Turkish cooks’ YouTube pages.

How About Outdoors?

Working full-time, studying hard and taking care of a family is tough, so I need some time to relax and feel life! I am so lucky to have a husband like mine when it comes to outdoor activities! We like to go hiking on weekends and for that, Western Washington is a paradise. There are parks, trails, and rain forest any direction we want to go. Looking at all the plants, bugs and animals is incredibly relaxing. After a long hike, a wonderful view and well-earned aches are very rewarding.

Whenever we have a week for ourselves, we like to travel. I think that our favorite place in the entire world is Kauai, where the sun, rain, shade, wind…everything seems to have been brought there from Heaven.

In that spirit, before you leave, please enjoy this traditional Hawaiian wedding song.
